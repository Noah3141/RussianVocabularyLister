The Verb Tree Model

The verb tree model presents Russian verbs as routinely derived sets of branch pairs, generated predictably from a small number of root space arrangements.

The footers "Root Space" and "Branch Space" are intended to be considered systematically paired. That is, a root space of (-ать/-нуть) should be assumed to create a doubled perfective branch, etc.
