3/28/2023

  • The verb pair page is now loaded at base with much of the manual deck I made (see The Tree Model), such that a good deal of the squirrel-y language processing from database only applies to the outskirts of vocabulary. This means the fundamentals are manual, and accurate, and the output is only left to complex and faulty processing where necessary. This isn't as cool from a natural langauge processing perspective, but it speeds up getting the app to a hyper-useful, very accurate level. Less coding, more manual, less errors, less time, more success. (Where doubled imperfective branches occur, preference was given to the -ать, -еть or -сти form over the -нуть)
  • Reflexives are in! And they have been sorted next to their transitive counterparts.
  • Fixed some of the correct Trees page entries being accompanied by a broken duplicate of themselves
  • The words in over 100 books were added to the database, hopefully making a dent in the amount of manual update necessary, and improving contents of total lists.
  • Attempts were made to fix the web app not loading using "russianvocabularylistmaker.com/" (as of now it still requires /rubit explicitly)
